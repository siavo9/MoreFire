How to Play

A raging fire breaks out. Tap the glowing base at the bottom of each flame to hit it with water. The flame above the base won't work — aim low!

Put out all 20 fires with your limited water before time runs out. Try 😈 Hard Mode for half the time and a fire that grows back on every miss!
