🚒 Privacy

Last updated: 2026

Stop The Fire is a small browser game. We respect your privacy and built this app to collect as little as possible.

What we collect

Nothing. Stop The Fire does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no sign-ups, no advertising, no third-party SDKs, and no analytics or tracking of any kind.

What is stored on your device

The game uses your browser's local storage only to remember:

  • Whether you have already seen the how-to-play tutorial.
  • Your sound on/off preference.
  • Your best scores (fastest save time and most water saved).

This data never leaves your device. Clearing your browser data (or uninstalling the app) removes it permanently.

Network requests

Once loaded, the game runs entirely offline thanks to a service worker. No network requests are made to any third-party server.

Children

Stop The Fire is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone — children included.

Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated date at the top of this page will be revised.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
